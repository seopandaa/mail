====================================
DNS RECORDS FOR fx.avameta.dev
====================================

IMPORTANT: Add these records to your DNS provider's control panel.
Replace [YOUR_VPS_IP] with your actual VPS IP address.
Replace [DKIM_PUBLIC_KEY] with the value from: sudo cat /etc/opendkim/keys/fx.avameta.dev/mail.txt

------------------------------------
1. A RECORD (if not already set)
------------------------------------
Type: A
Name: fx.avameta.dev (or @)
Value: [YOUR_VPS_IP]
TTL: 3600

------------------------------------
2. MX RECORD
------------------------------------
Type: MX
Name: @ (or fx.avameta.dev)
Value: fx.avameta.dev
Priority: 10
TTL: 3600

------------------------------------
3. SPF RECORD
------------------------------------
Type: TXT
Name: @ (or fx.avameta.dev)
Value: v=spf1 mx a ip4:[YOUR_VPS_IP] ~all
TTL: 3600

Example with IP:
v=spf1 mx a ip4:123.45.67.89 ~all

------------------------------------
4. DKIM RECORD
------------------------------------
Type: TXT
Name: mail._domainkey (or mail._domainkey.fx.avameta.dev)
Value: [DKIM_PUBLIC_KEY]
TTL: 3600

To get your DKIM public key, run:
sudo cat /etc/opendkim/keys/fx.avameta.dev/mail.txt

The output will look like:
mail._domainkey IN TXT ( "v=DKIM1; h=sha256; k=rsa; p=MIIBIjANBgkqhkiG9w0BAQEFAAOCAQ8AMIIBCgKCAQEA..." )

Copy only the part inside the quotes, for example:
v=DKIM1; h=sha256; k=rsa; p=MIIBIjANBgkqhkiG9w0BAQEFAAOCAQ8AMIIBCgKCAQEA...

If it's split across multiple lines, combine them into one line.

------------------------------------
5. DMARC RECORD
------------------------------------
Type: TXT
Name: _dmarc (or _dmarc.fx.avameta.dev)
Value: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc-reports@fx.avameta.dev; ruf=mailto:dmarc-failures@fx.avameta.dev; fo=1; adkim=r; aspf=r; pct=100; ri=86400
TTL: 3600

------------------------------------
6. PTR RECORD (Reverse DNS)
------------------------------------
This is configured through your VPS provider, not your domain DNS.

====================================
VERIFICATION
====================================

After adding DNS records, verify them:

1. Check MX record:
   nslookup -type=mx fx.avameta.dev

2. Check SPF record:
   nslookup -type=txt fx.avameta.dev

3. Check DKIM record:
   nslookup -type=txt mail._domainkey.fx.avameta.dev

4. Check DMARC record:
   nslookup -type=txt _dmarc.fx.avameta.dev

5. Test deliverability:
   https://www.mail-tester.com/
   Send an email to the provided address and check your score.
